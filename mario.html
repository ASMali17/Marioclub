It's a me, Mario

Mario is a fictional character created by Nintendo. He is one of the most iconic video game characters and has appeared in numerous games, cartoons, and movies. Mario made his first appearance in the arcade game Donkey Kong in 1981, where he was originally known as "Jumpman." However, he gained widespread popularity as the main character in the game Super Mario Bros., released for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) in 1985.Mario's popularity extends beyond video games. He has become a cultural icon and is recognized worldwide. The Mario franchise has also expanded into other media, such as animated TV shows and feature films.
